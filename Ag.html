Agathonisi is a small island in the Dodecanese island chain, it is not famous for anything to my knowledge due to it being so small

Agathonisi's charm is it's small size, the community, and the overall quietness of the island.

The main things to do on the island is explore and go to the beach, the main beach is mikro chorio, but I will also strongly recommend "Παραλία Σπηλιάς" which is much smaller but much nicer although there are a lot of sea urchins so be clear of that.

For food I would really recommend just exploring around to find a resturant. BUT it is super important to bring atleast 2L of water as the island has problems with that if there are many tourists.
